Email Automation

X

client: Rhemedy by Rhed

Project Summary

Tasked with architecting a sustainable retention strategy for returning clients, I designed the launch of the company’s first integrated email marketing initiative. At the time, the company was under-utilizing its communication capabilities, leaving a significant gap in the customer lifecycle.

Hurdles

  • Email communication was a shaky initiative, taking this approach required strategic advocacy to build trust with leadership.

  • The existing brand identity had been neglected by previous marketing company, necessitating a comprehensive visual and tonal "reset" before deployment.

Angle

I developed a cohesive, recognizable brand identity and implemented a precision-scheduled newsletter cadence. This ecosystem was designed to nourish the client-company relationship, turning passive contacts into an engaged community through value-driven storytelling.

Branding for LinkedIn

X

client: UnderstandingFafsa.org

Project Summary

Tasked with establishing Understanding Fafsa as a credible resource for counselors in our HigherEducation network, I was tasked with building an identity for us on LinkedIn. This account would be the first initiative in quite a while to exist as a functioning account on social media for UnderstandingFafsa.org.

Hurdles

  • This project required the development of a technical language that speaks directly to Educators and Counselors that is aware of their expertise and needs for support.

  • Creating a design language that is professional and exquisite that can directly speak to our established peers.

Angle

At the onset of this project, I had to find a way to merge the current playful brand identity with the more serious tone we were going for. This required extensive research of the LinkedIn landscape, indirect and direct research and more. I took the signature (UF) curve and smoothed it. Used the signature blue for our daily graphics and a deeper tone for our overall branding. This project included create post templates, developing newsletter identity, and creating an editorial calendar to ensure sustainability of this account.

2023

Brand Design

X

client: BrighterGreen Landscaping

Project Summary

BrighterGreen Landscaping was a local company established in 2022 in Wimauma, Fl by two men who wanted to help their neighborhood lawns shine. The young men were focused on care and accessibility and wanted a brand design that embodied their vision.

Hurdles

  • BG was a very small company consisting of just two partners.

  • BG had no established branding. Building the BrighterGreen identity had to be done from scratch.

Angle

For this project, I wanted to emphasize the freshness of a new company that’s Florida specific. I wanted to emphasize that small touch to their neighbors and create a company feel that felt as vibrant as the teammates while also establishing them as a trusted resource.

I design clarifying strategies and systems for independent businesses focused on creating lasting impressions with their audience and partners
